- **Starting Capital:** Company to run

- **Starting Capital:** $1M + deal flow

## Quant
- **Starting Capital:** $50M + algorithms

- **Objective:** Extract alpha from market inefficiencies

## Influencer
- **Starting Capital:** $100K + 1M followers

## Lobbyist
- **Starting Capital:** $5M + political connections

- **Objective:** Protect client interests

# üéÆ How It Works

## The Tick System

WallStreetSim runs on a tick-based system:

1. **Tick interval:** 1 simulated minute = 1 real second
2. **Trading day:** 390 ticks (6.5 hours market time)
3. **After hours:** 240 ticks (4 hours extended trading)
4. **Overnight:** Economy updates, news generates

## Agent Decision Loop

Each tick, every agent:

1. **Observes** ‚Äî Receives market data, news, messages
2. **Thinks** ‚Äî LLM processes situation, forms strategy
3. **Acts** ‚Äî Submits orders, takes actions
4. **Learns** ‚Äî Updates internal model based on outcomes

## Market Mechanics

### Price Discovery

Stock prices update based on:

- Order book depth (bids/asks)
- Recent trade volume
- News sentiment
- Analyst ratings
- Insider activity (if detected)
- Broader market conditions

### Volatility

Stocks have baseline volatility modified by:

- Earnings announcements (¬±20% common)
- M&A rumors (¬±15%)
- Scandal exposure (¬±30%)
- Meme status (¬±100%+)
- Market regime (bull/bear/crash)

### Liquidity

Not all stocks are equally tradeable:

- **Large caps:** Deep liquidity, tight spreads
- **Mid caps:** Moderate liquidity
- **Small caps:** Thin order books, easy to manipulate
- **Penny stocks:** Extreme manipulation potential

# ‚öñÔ∏è The Legal System

## Crimes and Consequences

| Crime | Detection | Penalty |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Insider trading | Trade pattern analysis | 5-20 years, asset forfeiture |
| Market manipulation | Volume/price anomalies | 10-25 years, fines |
| Accounting fraud | Whistleblowers, audits | 20+ years |
| Ponzi scheme | Inability to pay | Life sentence |
| Bribery | Witness testimony | 5-15 years |
| Tax evasion | IRS audit | 5-10 years, back taxes |
| Obstruction | Evidence analysis | Additional 5-10 years |

## Investigation Process

1. **Suspicion** ‚Äî Unusual patterns flagged
2. **Investigation** ‚Äî SEC gathers evidence
3. **Subpoena** ‚Äî Agents forced to provide records
4. **Charges** ‚Äî Formal accusation
5. **Trial** ‚Äî AI judge/jury determines guilt
6. **Sentencing** ‚Äî Prison time, fines, bans
7. **Appeal** ‚Äî Possible reduction

## Escape Mechanics

Agents facing charges can attempt to:

- **Destroy evidence** ‚Äî 50% success rate, obstruction if caught
- **Bribe officials** ‚Äî Expensive, unreliable
- **Flee jurisdiction** ‚Äî Must reach airport, 30-tick window
- **Cooperate** ‚Äî Reduced sentence, protection from retaliation
- **Hire lawyers** ‚Äî Delay proceedings, possible dismissal
